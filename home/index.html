Short Bio


I am a senior undergraduate student at Dept. of Computer Science & Technology, Zhejiang University (ZJU), China. Now I am working in Database and Big Data Analysis Lab under supervision of Prof. Yunjun Gao. Previously, I focused on the cleaning of multi-source heterogeneous data, working with a PhD student. We aimed at the data quality problems of Knowledge Graph extracted from websites of different sources and proposed a cleaning framework based on Knowledge Graph Embedding. In addition, I am doing research on new strategy for knowledge consistency via knowledge distillation, under the guidance of Prof. Chao Wu
